Classical Ayurvedic texts contain rich domain knowledge, but that knowledge is difficult to access computationally because it is embedded in dense, linguistically complex source material. This project focuses on building a **structured question-answer dataset** from *Bhavaprakasha Nighantu*, grounded in an annotated Ayurvedic knowledge graph.

The work involves studying source chapters in detail, drafting domain-faithful QA templates, and preserving critical technical terminology such as *Rasa*, *Guna*, *Virya*, and *Vipaka* so that the resulting dataset is useful for downstream NLP and educational applications.

The broader goal is to make classical Ayurvedic knowledge searchable and learnable through intelligent systems, while maintaining linguistic and domain-specific fidelity.
