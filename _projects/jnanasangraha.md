**Jñānasaṅgraha** is a collection of computational applications related to Sanskrit, hosted at [sanskrit.iitk.ac.in/jnanasangraha](https://sanskrit.iitk.ac.in/jnanasangraha/). It brings together tools and utilities for processing, analysing, and interacting with Sanskrit text, intended for both researchers and learners.
