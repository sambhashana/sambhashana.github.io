Focused projects probing LLMs in areas where failure modes are poorly understood or where stakes are high.

**Math & LLMs** — Where and why do LLMs fail at mathematical reasoning? Investigating systematic failure patterns across problem types and exploring whether targeted interventions can make LLMs more reliable on formal problem-solving.

**Team:** Ruchi Harge, Snehal Reddy

**Security & LLMs** — Adversarial robustness, prompt injection, and safety in security-sensitive deployments. Studying how LLMs behave under adversarial input and what defences transfer across attack types.

**Team:** Amol Agrawal

**Multi-turn Prompting** — LLM behaviour in conversation drifts in ways that single-turn evaluations miss. Studying how consistency, instruction-following, and factual accuracy degrade (or hold) over multi-turn exchanges.

**Team:** Anshuman Sharma
