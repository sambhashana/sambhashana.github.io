**Vaiyyākaraṇa** is a Sanskrit grammar bot available on Telegram ([@vyakarana_bot](https://t.me/vyakarana_bot)). It provides interactive grammar assistance for Sanskrit — useful for students, researchers, and enthusiasts looking up grammatical forms, rules, and derivations.

### Future Work

- Expanding coverage to more grammatical features and derivational forms
- Support for additional platforms (Discord, web interface)
- Integration with digitised Sanskrit texts for in-context grammar lookup
