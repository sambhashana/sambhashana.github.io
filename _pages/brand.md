## Name

The research group is named **Saṁbhāṣaṇa** (Sanskrit: संभाषण, meaning *conversation* or *dialogue*) — apt for a group whose work is fundamentally about enabling machines to understand, generate, and mediate human language.

When diacritics are not available, the romanized spelling **Sambhashana** is acceptable.

The group is officially named **Saṁbhāṣaṇa Research Group** and is based at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus.

## Usage Guidelines

- Use the full group name on first mention in a document or poster; the short form *Sambhashana* is fine thereafter.
- Do not stretch, recolor, or add effects to the logo.
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the lettermark.
- When in doubt, use the canonical files from the
  [brand-assets repository](https://github.com/sambhashana/brand-assets).
