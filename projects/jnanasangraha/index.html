Jñānasaṅgraha

A collection of computational applications for Sanskrit

Jñānasaṅgraha is a collection of computational applications related to Sanskrit, hosted at sanskrit.iitk.ac.in/jnanasangraha. It brings together tools and utilities for processing, analysing, and interacting with Sanskrit text, intended for both researchers and learners.

(Terdalkar & Bhattacharya, 2023)
