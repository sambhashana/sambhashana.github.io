Delivered a talk at the [India AI Summit 2026 Pre-Summit](https://appcair.bits-pilani.ac.in/pre-summit-india-ai-summit-2026.html), organized by APPCAIR, BITS Pilani in coordination with the Government of India. The pre-summit brought together researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to discuss the state and future of AI in India, with a focus on language diversity, accessibility, and responsible deployment of AI systems in Indian contexts.
